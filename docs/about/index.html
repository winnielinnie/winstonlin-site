About

About me.

A bit more on my background and how I like to work:

I'm based in San Francisco and work across cloud products, developer tools, AI workflows, and business systems. Most of my work has lived in the part of the job where direction, constraints, and day-to-day execution all meet.

I like getting from abstraction to something real: a clearer workflow, a sharper decision, a better handoff, or a small tool that saves people time. I use AI pretty hands-on as part of that, usually with real files, scripts, and review loops rather than one-off prompting.

I'm comfortable working across engineers, operators, leadership, and business stakeholders. Outside the day job, I also advise and invest in a small set of businesses and startups in the U.S. and Asia.

Background

Where I come from.

I grew up between Taipei and Orange County, which made me comfortable moving between different environments early.

I later earned a B.S. from UC Berkeley Haas and got more interested in the overlap between business and technology. I also spent a lot of time tutoring, mentoring, and working in lab assistant roles, and stayed involved after college as a mentor for the undergraduate program for a few years after.

Competitive math and community volunteering were also a big part of how I grew up. That mix of analytical work and helping people still shapes how I think and how I like to work.
